Become a Sitter

How to Sign Up

To become a pet sitter on our platform, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the header.
  2. Fill out the required information in the sign-up form, including your full name, email address, password, phone number, and address.
  3. Provide details about your experience with pets, including the number of years, types of pets you've cared for, and your availability.
  4. Upload a copy of your eligibility certificate to verify your work experience.
  5. Submit the form and wait for verification.

How to Create a Profile

Once your account is verified, you can create your pet sitter profile:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Click on the "Profile" section in the navigation menu.
  3. Fill out the profile form with details about yourself, your pet care experience, services you offer, and any other relevant information.
  4. Upload a profile picture to personalize your profile.
  5. Save your changes.

Verification Process

Our team will review your application and verify your eligibility as a pet sitter. This process may take up to 3 business days.

Once verified, your profile will be listed on our platform, and pet owners can contact you for pet sitting services.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact us.
