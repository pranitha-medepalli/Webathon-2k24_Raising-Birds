About Us

Welcome to Pet Sitter Connect, your go-to platform for connecting pet owners with trusted pet sitters in their local community.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and reliable solution for pet owners who need someone to care for their furry friends while they're away, and for individuals who are looking to earn extra income by pet sitting.

Vision

We envision a world where every pet owner can find a trustworthy and caring pet sitter, and where pet sitters are empowered to provide the best possible care for their furry clients.
